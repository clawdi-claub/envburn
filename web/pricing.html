Questions

How does the encryption work?
Secrets are encrypted with NaCl (TweetNaCl.js) entirely in your browser before being sent to our server. The decryption key lives in the URL fragment and never reaches our server.
What happens when a secret is viewed?
If burn-after-read is enabled (1 view), the encrypted data is permanently deleted from our database after the first view. Multi-view secrets decrement until they hit zero.
Can I cancel Pro anytime?
Yes. Pro is billed monthly through Stripe. Cancel anytime and you keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
